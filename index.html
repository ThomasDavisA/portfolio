Kobold Cave

Wake the dragon! A small incremental game I made before joining the Thinkful bootcamp, using vanilla JS/HTML/CSS. Starting with pushing a button, Kobold Cave blossomed into rudimentary AI for the kobolds that you manage as they do their day to day task of gathering more coins for the sleeping beast. I learned how to handle many objects and fundamental logic. This is in continual development.
