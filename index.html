About Me

Hey everyone, I'm a developer with a talent for persistence and a desire to make joy in people's lives. I love video games with a passion and enjoy analyzing the systems within them to discover what makes them so enjoyable. Bringing people together and making them smile is what I do best, and I rarely stop trying to achieve that goal.

I work on making small games when I am not investing time into playing them, and I draw and model on the side as a quiet hobby. Having an artistic side of me helps me design frontends that delight others.

I am also pretty good at fighting games. I enjoy the competition, and I've attended a tournament once. I intend to commit more time in the future to one day attend Evo.
