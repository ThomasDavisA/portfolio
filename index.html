Projects

Dragon Hoard

Dragon Hoard is a project I am currently working on using the SAMMI program. Users are able to connect to the Twitch API and use their channel points to give a coin to a growing pile of coins that are tracked over the course of several streams. Dragon Hoard tracks not only the coins that have been given, divided into multiple rarities, but also tracks each person and what they have given to the hoard as well. They are also able to accrue their own custom portrait when giving a coin to display themselves on the stream.

Cursr

Cursr is a 'vent' app targeted toward adults and is meant to be used when a user is angry. I lead a team of developers in designing and implementing this simple yet powerful app, letting users curse into a void, where others will get to read these messages and send back a 'blessing', all of it completely anonymous. I largely handled styling and product management, keeping the team on task and on track in our three week period together. The idea came about when I was frustrated and could not simply vent it out.

SpeakEasy

Trying to learn a new language? This spaced repetition app was developed with me and two others using React and Node, while handling new data structures, and is targeted toward adults looking to learn a new language. We were able to get voice functionality as well as multiple-choice questions put into the App, all the while giving it a very calm and easy style for people to feel comfortable with. We wanted to build an app that lets users, as well as myself, learn a new language and to get comfortable with new vocabulary.
